What You Can Do

Built for Real-World Connection

POP turns the physical world into a social platform. Create location-anchored spaces, discover what's happening around you, and be part of the moment.

Drop a bubble on the map — public, private, or invite-only. Ephemeral or permanent. Your space, your rules.

Posts & Conversations

Share text, images, and videos inside bubbles. Start polls, spark discussions, and engage with real-time comments.

Direct & Group Chat

Message friends one-on-one or create group chats with images. Stay connected beyond the bubble.

Power & Reputation

Earn power through engagement. Climb the leaderboard. Your contributions build real influence within communities.

Our Vision

Social Without the Feed

We believe social media should bring people together in the real world — not keep them scrolling alone. POP replaces the infinite feed with a living map. Every bubble is a place. Every post is a moment. Every connection is grounded in shared space and time.
